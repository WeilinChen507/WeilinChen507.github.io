Biography

Weilin Chen is a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Computer Science at Guangdong University of Technology, working with Professor Ruichu Cai. He received a B.S. degree in Software Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology in 2020, and completed his Ph.D. study in Computer Science there under the supervision of Professor Ruichu Cai. From June to December 2024, he was a visiting student at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Professor Jose Miguel Hernandez-Lobato.

His research focuses on causal inference and causal discovery, especially long-term effect estimation and networked effect estimation. His work has appeared in machine learning venues and journals including ICML, WWW, IJCAI, AAAI, Neural Networks, and IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems.
