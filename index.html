Biography

Weilin Chen is a Boya postdoctoral researcher at Peking University, working with Professor Zhouchen Lin. Previously, from June 2025 to May 2026, he was a postdoctoral researcher at Guangdong University of Technology, working with Professor Ruichu Cai. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Guangdong University of Technology in 2025, supervised by Professor Ruichu Cai, and his B.S. degree in Software Engineering from the same university in 2020. From June to December 2024, he was a visiting student at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Professor Jose Miguel Hernandez-Lobato.

His research focuses on causal inference, causal discovery, and related applications, especially long-term causal effect estimation and networked effect estimation. His work has been published in venues including ICML, WWW, IJCAI, AAAI, ICLR, CVPR, Neural Networks, and TNNLS.
